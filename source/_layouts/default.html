About Lucas

The story of an irresponsible thirty year old man-child who decided he didn't want to wait until he was old to have a turn at enjoying retirement.

Instead of following the common life path of getting married, buying a house, and having two kids, he's chosen to seek out epic adventures, see the world, and hopefully have great times that he'll reminisce about when he's old and senile.
